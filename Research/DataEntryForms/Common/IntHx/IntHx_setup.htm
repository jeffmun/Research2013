How to add the Autism website to Internet Explorer's Intranet zone.
1. Select Internet Options... from the Tools menu.
2. Click on the Security tab.
3. Click on Local intranet zone then click Sites...
4. Click the Advanced... button.
5. Enter https://autism.washington.edu/ and click Add. Also check the 'Require server verification' box.  Click Ok on this form and all the previous ones.
